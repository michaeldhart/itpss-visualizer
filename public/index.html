Visualizing the ITPSS

The Illinois Department of Transportation has released its 2019 Illinois Traffic and Pedestrian Stop Study. This study provides valuable information about local police departments and may help to determine whether or not each department exhibits any racial bias in their traffic stop practices.

In my conversations, it seemed that there was a lot of confusion on what exactly the data is telling us. We're not all data scientists and complex data sets can be hard to interpret. Sometimes making data more visual helps us to understand what's going on in the numbers. That is the purpose of this site.

Conclusion

The degree to which racial bias exists in this municipality, according to the data of this study, is reflected in the above charts. How similar are Figure 2 and Figure 3? Figure 1 and Figure 4? The more similar they are, the less racial bias is presented by the data. The more different they are, the more racial bias is presented by the data.
